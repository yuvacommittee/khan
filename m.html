About us

Who we are

In 1948, Late Shri Ramji Dass Mongia started rice trading at Lahori Gate, Khari Baoli, Old Delhi, India. An expert in identifying the best quality rice along with an uncanny ability to sell, he expanded his business and reached par with the eminent Basmati rice suppliers in India, during his era.
